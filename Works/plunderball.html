Plunder Ball

11-27-2023

Plunder Ball was a year-long endeavour with a fluctuating team of 3-10 people in Unreal Engine 5. Dalton's main role was as a producer, as well as going through the process of handling the entire publishing process on Steam. Additionally, at the end of production, Dalton was working in Unreal Engine 5, in both C++ and blueprints, to address a number of unshippable and unpalatable bugs before release.

Contributions:
  • Producing
  • C++
  • Unreal Blueprints
  • Publishing to Steam
