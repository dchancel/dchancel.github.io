Plasticity

05-24-2019

Plasticity was an undergraduate senior capstone project in the Interactive Media and Games Division at the University of Southern California from 2018-2019. Dalton was the Usability Lead and spent about a year holding playtests, recording data, and communicating both good and bad news to the rest of the development team. He was additionally able to contribute to game design, narrative, and marketing discussions.

Contributions:
  • Usability Testing
  • Game Design
  • Narrative Design
  • Marketing
